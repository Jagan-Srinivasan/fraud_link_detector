About This Project

Fraud Link Detector helps you instantly check any shopping or suspicious link for scams, malware, or phishing threats. Powered by pattern analysis, VirusTotal, Google Safe Browsing, SSL/WHOIS, and more.

How It Works

  1. Paste or scan a link above.
  2. Our AI and security APIs check for common fraud patterns, blacklist status, SSL, and domain trust.
  3. Get a clear, color-coded verdict and risk suggestions.
